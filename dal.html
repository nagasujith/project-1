Dal Recipe

Servings: 4

Contains: Lentils, Spices

Ingredients

  • 1 cup yellow or red lentils (masoor dal or toor dal)
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced

Instructions

  1. Rinse the lentils and soak them in water for about 30 minutes.
  2. In a pot, heat oil and sauté the chopped onions until they turn golden brown.
  3. Add minced garlic and sauté for a minute.
  4. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they soften.
  5. Drain the soaked lentils and add them to the pot.
  6. Add water and bring to a boil. Skim off any foam that forms on the surface.
  7. Reduce the heat, cover, and let the lentils simmer until they are tender and fully cooked.
  8. Season with spices and salt according to taste.
  9. For added flavor, you can temper the dal with ghee, cumin seeds, and other spices.
  10. Serve the dal hot with rice or bread.
