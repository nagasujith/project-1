Avocado Toast Recipe

Calories: 290

Contains: Protein, Fat, Sodium

Ingredients

  • 2 slices whole-grain bread
  • 1 ripe avocado, pitted and peeled
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Pinch of salt and pepper

Instructions

  1. Toast the slices of bread until golden brown.
  2. Mash the ripe avocado with a fork and spread it onto the toasted bread.
  3. Drizzle olive oil over the avocado and sprinkle with salt and pepper.
  4. Enjoy your delicious and nutritious Avocado Toast!
