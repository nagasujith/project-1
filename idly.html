Idly Recipe

Calories: 35 to 39

Contains: Potassium, Carbohydrates, Sodium

Ingredients

  • 1 cup idly rice
  • 1/4 cup urad dal (black gram)
  • 1/4 teaspoon fenugreek seeds
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Wash and soak the idly rice, urad dal, and fenugreek seeds separately for about 4-6 hours.
  2. Grind the soaked rice, urad dal, and fenugreek seeds into a smooth batter.
  3. Add salt and mix the batter well. Allow it to ferment overnight or for 6-8 hours.
  4. Grease the idly molds and pour the batter into each mold.
  5. Steam the idlies in a steamer for about 10-15 minutes or until they are cooked and fluffy.
  6. Remove the idlies from the molds and serve hot with chutney and sambar.
