Vegetable Biryani Recipe

Servings: 6

Contains: Basmati Rice, Vegetables, Spices

Ingredients

  • 2 cups Basmati rice
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (carrots, peas, beans, potatoes), chopped
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1/4 cup plain yogurt

Instructions

  1. Rinse the Basmati rice under cold water until the water runs clear. Soak the rice for 30 minutes.
  2. In a large pot, heat oil and sauté the thinly sliced onions until they turn golden brown.
  3. Add the chopped tomatoes and cook until they soften.
  4. Add the mixed vegetables and cook for a few minutes.
  5. Drain the soaked rice and add it to the pot. Stir gently to combine.
  6. Add yogurt and a blend of biryani spices. Mix well.
  7. Add water and bring to a boil. Reduce the heat, cover, and let the biryani simmer until the rice is cooked and fluffy.
  8. Fluff the biryani gently with a fork. Serve hot, garnished with fresh herbs and fried onions.
