Khichdi Recipe

Calories: 250

Contains: Rice, Lentils, Spices

Ingredients

  • 1 cup rice
  • 1/2 cup lentils (such as moong dal or masoor dal)
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Wash the rice and lentils together, and soak them for 30 minutes.
  2. In a pot, heat some oil and add cumin seeds. Let them splutter.
  3. Add the soaked rice and lentils, turmeric powder, and salt. Mix well.
  4. Add water (about 4 cups) and bring to a boil. Reduce heat to low, cover, and let it simmer until rice and lentils are cooked and the mixture has a porridge-like consistency.
  5. You can adjust the consistency by adding more water if needed.
  6. Serve hot with ghee and your choice of side dishes.
