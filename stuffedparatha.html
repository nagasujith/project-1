Stuffed Paratha Recipe

Servings: 4

Contains: Whole Wheat Flour, Vegetable Filling

Ingredients

  • 2 cups whole wheat flour
  • 1 cup mixed vegetable filling (e.g., spiced potatoes, paneer)
  • 1 teaspoon oil or ghee
  • Water (as needed)

Instructions

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine whole wheat flour, oil or ghee, and a pinch of salt.
  2. Add water gradually and knead to form a smooth dough.
  3. Divide the dough and filling into equal-sized portions.
  4. Take a portion of dough and roll it into a small disc.
  5. Place a portion of filling in the center of the disc and seal the edges to form a ball.
  6. Roll out the stuffed ball into a round paratha on a floured surface.
  7. Heat a tawa or skillet over medium heat.
  8. Cook the rolled paratha on the hot tawa, flipping and cooking both sides until golden brown spots appear.
  9. Apply ghee or oil on both sides while cooking.
  10. Repeat the process for the remaining dough and filling portions.
