Roti Sabzi Recipe

Servings: 4

Contains: Vegetables, Spices

Ingredients

  • 4 whole wheat rotis (Indian bread)
  • 2 potatoes, peeled and cubed
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (carrots, peas, beans), chopped
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped

Instructions

  1. In a pan, heat oil and sauté the chopped onions until they turn golden brown.
  2. Add the cubed potatoes and mixed vegetables. Cook until they are partially tender.
  3. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they soften and form a thick gravy.
  4. Add spices and seasonings according to taste.
  5. Warm the whole wheat rotis on a tava (griddle).
  6. Serve the vegetable curry (sabzi) hot with the warm rotis.
