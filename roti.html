Roti Recipe

Calories: 100

Contains: Carbohydrates, Fiber

Ingredients

  • 2 cups whole wheat flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • Water (as needed)

Instructions

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine whole wheat flour and salt.
  2. Add water gradually and knead to form a soft and smooth dough.
  3. Divide the dough into small equal-sized balls.
  4. Roll each ball into a thin, round roti on a floured surface.
  5. Heat a tawa or skillet over medium-high heat.
  6. Place the rolled roti on the hot tawa and cook until bubbles appear on the surface.
  7. Flip the roti and cook the other side until golden brown spots appear.
  8. Remove from the tawa and apply a little ghee or butter (optional).
  9. Repeat the process for the remaining dough balls.
