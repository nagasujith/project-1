Curd Rice Recipe

Servings: 2

Contains: Rice, Yogurt

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cooked rice
  • 1 cup plain yogurt (curd)
  • 1/4 cup milk (optional)
  • 1 cucumber, finely chopped

Instructions

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine the cooked rice and plain yogurt.
  2. If desired, add milk to adjust the consistency.
  3. Add finely chopped cucumber and mix well.
  4. Season with salt to taste and mix again.
  5. Refrigerate the curd rice for a couple of hours to allow flavors to meld.
  6. Serve the chilled curd rice as a refreshing side dish.

Indulge in the soothing simplicity of Curd Rice, a beloved South Indian dish that harmoniously combines the creaminess of yogurt with the comfort of rice. This humble creation marries the coolness of yogurt with the mildness of cooked rice, resulting in a dish that's as comforting as it is nourishing. The rice is enrobed in a velvety coat of yogurt, creating a delightful contrast between the soft grains and the creamy texture. A touch of milk can be added for extra richness, if desired. The addition of finely chopped cucumber adds a refreshing crunch, infusing the dish with a burst of freshness. A pinch of salt ties the flavors together, while the coolness of the dish provides respite from the heat. Whether enjoyed on its own or as a accompaniment to spicy dishes, Curd Rice is a delightful reminder that sometimes, the most cherished culinary experiences arise from the simplest of ingredients.
