Dosa Recipe

Calories: 168

Contains: Potassium, Carbohydrates, Sodium

Ingredients

  • 1 cup rice
  • 1/2 cup urad dal (black gram)
  • 1/4 teaspoon fenugreek seeds
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Wash and soak the rice, urad dal, and fenugreek seeds separately for about 4-6 hours.
  2. Grind the soaked rice, urad dal, and fenugreek seeds into a smooth batter.
  3. Add salt and mix the batter well. Allow it to ferment overnight or for 6-8 hours.
  4. Heat a non-stick pan and pour a ladleful of batter in the center.
  5. Spread the batter in a circular motion to make a thin dosa. Drizzle oil around the edges.
  6. Cook until the dosa turns golden brown and crispy.
  7. Serve the dosa hot with chutney and sambar.
