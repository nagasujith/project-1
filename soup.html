Vegetable Soup Recipe

Servings: 4

Contains: Mixed Vegetables, Broth

Ingredients

  • 2 cups mixed vegetables (carrots, celery, peas, corn), chopped
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 4 cups vegetable broth

Instructions

  1. In a large pot, heat oil and sauté the diced onions until translucent.
  2. Add the minced garlic and sauté for another minute.
  3. Add the mixed vegetables and cook until slightly softened.
  4. Pour in the vegetable broth and bring to a simmer.
  5. Let the soup simmer for about 15-20 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld.
  6. Using an immersion blender, carefully blend the soup to your desired consistency.
  7. Season the soup with salt, pepper, and any desired herbs or spices.
  8. Serve the vegetable soup hot, garnished with fresh herbs if desired.
