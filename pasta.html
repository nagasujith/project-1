Pasta Recipe

Servings: 2

Contains: Pasta, Sauce, Vegetables (Optional)

Ingredients

  • 8 oz (220g) pasta
  • 1 cup pasta sauce (marinara, Alfredo, or your choice)
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish

Instructions

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil and cook the pasta according to the package instructions.
  2. Drain the pasta and return it to the pot.
  3. Warm the pasta sauce in a separate saucepan.
  4. Pour the sauce over the cooked pasta and toss to coat evenly.
  5. Serve the pasta in bowls and top with grated Parmesan cheese.
  6. Garnish with fresh basil leaves for added flavor.
