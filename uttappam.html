Uttappam Recipe

Calories: 120

Contains: Protein, Carbohydrates, Fiber

Ingredients

  • 1 cup dosa batter
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped onion
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped bell peppers
  • 1/4 cup grated carrot
  • 2-3 finely chopped green chilies
  • Handful of chopped cilantro
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Heat a non-stick skillet or tawa over medium heat.
  2. Grease the tawa with a little oil or cooking spray.
  3. Pour a ladleful of dosa batter onto the tawa and spread it in a circular motion to form a thick pancake.
  4. Sprinkle chopped onion, tomatoes, bell peppers, grated carrot, green chilies, and cilantro on top.
  5. Press the toppings gently into the batter with a spatula.
  6. Drizzle a little oil around the edges of the uttappam.
  7. Cook for a few minutes until the bottom is golden and crispy.
  8. Flip the uttappam and cook the other side until it's cooked through.
  9. Remove from the tawa and serve hot with chutney or sambar.

As the uttappam cooks, the toppings meld with the batter, infusing each bite with a burst of flavors and textures. The result is a scrumptious creation that boasts the heartiness of a pancake and the playfulness of a pizza. Uttappam is often enjoyed as a satisfying breakfast or a light meal, accompanied by coconut chutney or tangy sambar.
