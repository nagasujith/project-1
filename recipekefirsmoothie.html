Kefir Smoothie Recipe

Calories: 120

Contains: Vitamin A, Fiber

Ingredients

  • 1 cup kefir
  • 1 banana
  • 1/2 cup mixed berries
  • 1 tablespoon honey

Instructions

  1. Blend all the ingredients until smooth.
  2. Pour into a glass and enjoy!
