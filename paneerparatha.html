Paneer Paratha Recipe

Calories: 300

Contains: Paneer, Whole Wheat Flour, Spices

Ingredients

  • 2 cups whole wheat flour
  • 200g paneer (cottage cheese), crumbled
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine whole wheat flour and a pinch of salt.
  2. Add water gradually and knead to form a soft dough. Let it rest for 15-20 minutes.
  3. In a separate bowl, mix crumbled paneer, cumin seeds, red chili powder, turmeric powder, and salt.
  4. Divide the dough and paneer mixture into small equal-sized balls.
  5. Take a portion of dough and roll it into a small circle. Place a portion of paneer mixture in the center.
  6. Bring the edges of the dough together to cover the paneer mixture and seal the paratha.
  7. Roll out the stuffed dough into a flat, round paratha on a floured surface.
  8. Cook the paratha on a hot tawa or skillet, applying oil or ghee on both sides until golden brown spots appear.
  9. Repeat the process for the remaining dough and paneer mixture.
