Fried Rice Recipe

Servings: 4

Contains: Rice, Vegetables, Protein (Optional)

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked rice (preferably cooled)
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (e.g., carrots, peas, bell peppers)
  • 1/2 cup protein (e.g., cooked chicken, shrimp, tofu)
  • 2 eggs, beaten

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat.
  2. Add mixed vegetables and stir-fry until slightly tender.
  3. If using protein, add and stir-fry until cooked or heated through.
  4. Push vegetables and protein to one side of the skillet.
  5. Pour beaten eggs into the other side of the skillet and scramble until cooked.
  6. Add cooked rice to the skillet and stir-fry with vegetables, protein, and eggs.
  7. Season with soy sauce, salt, and pepper to taste.
  8. Continue stir-frying until everything is well mixed and heated through.
  9. Garnish with chopped green onions or cilantro if desired.
